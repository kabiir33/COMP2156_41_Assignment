COMP 2156 is a DevOps course offered at George Brown College in Toronto, Canada. It is designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to understand and implement DevOps practices in software development and IT operations. The course typically covers topics such as continuous integration, continuous delivery, infrastructure as code, configuration management, monitoring, and collaboration tools. Students may also learn about cloud computing platforms, containerization technologies like Docker, and automation tools such as Jenkins or Ansible. Through hands-on projects and practical exercises, students gain experience in setting up development pipelines, deploying applications, managing infrastructure, and improving team collaboration, preparing them for careers in DevOps roles in various industries.
