Learning DevOps engineering in the software development cycle is essential for streamlining processes, enhancing collaboration, and accelerating delivery. DevOps practices enable teams to automate tasks, implement continuous integration and deployment pipelines, and foster a culture of collaboration between development and operations. By embracing DevOps principles, organizations can improve the reliability, scalability, and efficiency of their software systems, leading to reduced downtime, faster time-to-market, and better alignment with business objectives, ultimately driving overall success in today's fast-paced digital landscape.
